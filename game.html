ISRO Space Program

Here is a small fun game of Missing Rocket, floating in space with no way home, at the boundary of space, high above planet Kerbin. Loosely, the goal of the game is for players to move the missing rocket in space and kill aliens, have fun with this small game.
Welcome to the game Space Rocket Program where you get to run your own space agency and decide everything from mission parameters to the design of the ships. It’s as much fun to fail as it is to succeed.
It is a virtual space race at your fingertips. The idea is that any person can play the game and have fun. The idea for this game has suggested by our team and it was an imaginative. That dream, the fun of trying to achieve something big with so little, never getting discouraged, but simply asking why .“Make your dream come true” was a program for it’s employees where they could present any idea they had to the company and if the bosses liked it, they’d come on board and help to realize that dream if possible.

INSTRUCTONS: Space Rocket Program gives you an inside view on what it is like to fly your own rocket and kill aliens, use left and right arrow keys for the movement of cannon and press space key to shoot.
WARNING: Be safe from Aliens Attack.
