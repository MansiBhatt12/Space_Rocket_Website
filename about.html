ABOUT US

Space Rocket Organisation Media Foundation is a private, not-for-profit organisation established for the purpose of fostering science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) education throughout countries.This is our first project is the publication of blends space history-past,present and future – with astronomy lessons, aerospace and news, vedio features, technology spinoffs, and educational resources; along with explanations of the science and astronomy of all things to do with space travel.

Our Goals:

  • Inspire the world’s next generation of scientists, engineers and astronauts.
  • Keep educators informed on space developments and help them better work STEM lessons into their classrooms.
  • Raise awareness of the benefits of space exploration among all citizens of planet Earth.
  • Encourage international cooperation in space exploration.
